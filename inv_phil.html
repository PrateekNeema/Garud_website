Garud IM

Systematic Investing with a Focus on Risk-Adjusted Excellence

Garud Investments is a boutique asset management firm committed to delivering superior long-term outcomes through a disciplined, systematic approach to equity investing. Our philosophy is rooted in data-driven decision-making, where every investment action is governed by a well-defined process—free from emotional or discretionary influence. We focus exclusively on achieving high risk-adjusted returns for our clients by employing structured methods that are continuously researched, tested, and refined over time.

We believe that consistent investment performance stems not from market timing or speculation, but from applying a repeatable framework built on logic, transparency, and control. Our operations are designed around long-term alignment with our investors, ethical integrity in execution, and a deep respect for capital stewardship. Garud is intentionally selective in its investor base, offering access only to those who value strategic discipline, are comfortable with volatility, and appreciate a rational approach to wealth creation.

We realize this vision by bringing together multidisciplinary knowledge, state-of-the-art technology, and the timeless principles of sound fund management. We are essentially a research and development lab constantly working to produce globally competitive models that form the foundation of our strategies.

What sets us apart is our team — a strong mix of youthful energy and seasoned experience. Our investor-first mindset ensures that every decision is made with the client's best interest in mind, backed by a commitment to the highest standards of ethics. Right now, we’re excited to be launching an Alternative Investment Fund (AIF) scheme built on a world-class automated model, taking a big step forward in our mission.
