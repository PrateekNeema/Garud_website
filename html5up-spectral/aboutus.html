About Us

An All-Star Team, Built for the Future of Investing

Sachin Neema

Partner, Investment Strategy | CFA, MMS – JBIMS, Mechanical Engineer – VJTI

With over 28 years of experience in Indian equity markets, Sachin Neema brings a deep well of institutional knowledge, strategic vision, and portfolio management expertise. He has held senior leadership positions across India’s top investment management firms—serving as Senior Fund Manager at ICICI Prudential PMS, Head of Portfolio Management Services at L&T and UTI, and in various leadership roles across HDFC Securities, IIFL, Tata, Prime, and Edelweiss.

Sachin’s career spans every facet of equity markets—from equity research and stock selection to heading business verticals and managing large AUMs. His experience on both the buy and sell sides gives him a uniquely holistic view of the investment landscape. At Garud, he anchors the fund's strategic direction and risk framework, bringing decades of market-tested judgment and a reputation built on trust, integrity, and consistent performance.
